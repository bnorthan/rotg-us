HMRRC Paypal Troubleshooting

HMRRC Paypal Troubleshooting

Step 1

  • If you have a PayPal account
      select "Have a PayPal account?"
  • Otherwise fill in the form that is below the words
      • "Don't have a PayPal account?"
  • Make sure all information is correct.

HMRRC Paypal Troubleshooting

Step 2

  • Fill in credit card and/or debit card information
  • MAKE SURE BILLING ADDRESS IS CORRECT
    • Change Billing Address if incorrect
  • Make sure expiration date and CSC number are correct.

HMRRC Paypal Troubleshooting

If something goes wrong

  • Your e-mail address may be associated with a defunct PayPal account
    • try a different e-mail if possible
  • Transcribe error message exactly and send to HMRRC representative
  • Take a screen shot of the error message and send to HMRRC representative
